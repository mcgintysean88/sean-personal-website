**Reading**  
My reading life is pretty all over the place — and I like it that way. I'm drawn to big adventure and exploration stories like *Endurance*, *River of Doubt*, and *Undaunted Courage*, and I have a soft spot for anything rooted in the American South, especially Pat Conroy. Growing up in the Lowcountry means books like *The Great Santini*, *Prince of Tides*, and *A Lowcountry Heart* hit differently for me. I also tend to gravitate toward narrative history and biography — *Titan*, *Devil in the White City*, and *Rebel Yell* are the kind of books I find myself recommending. On the nonfiction side, I enjoy anything that makes me think differently about decisions, business, or human behavior, whether that's *Thinking in Bets*, *The Big Short*, or *Zero to One*. Throw in the occasional novel (*Dune*, *The Count of Monte Cristo*, *Corelli's Mandolin*) and that's pretty much my reading life in a nutshell.
